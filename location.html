Parking & Transportation

Parking

Street parking is available surrounding streets. A public parking garage is located one block east on cozy street.

Public Transit

Bus stop directly in front of our café. The nearest subway station is Metro 12 - Saint-Georges, a 2-minute walk away, and 5-minute away from Metro 2 - Pigalle.

Cycling

Bike racks are available directly in front of our café.

Our Neighborhood

Nestled in the vibrant 9th arrondissement, Shigure Café is a peaceful retreat surrounded by art, history, and Parisian charm. While you're visiting, we invite you to explore these nearby gems:

Musée Gustave Moreau

Immerse yourself in the world of Symbolist painter Gustave Moreau at his former home and studio, just a short stroll from our door.

Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur

Wander up the hill to the iconic Montmartre district, where artists sketch in the Place du Tertre and the Sacré-Cœur Basilica offers breathtaking views of Paris.

Opéra Garnier & Grands Magasins

Experience the grandeur of the Opéra Garnier and indulge in a world-class shopping experience at the nearby Grands Magasins, Printemps and Galeries Lafayette.

Place Pigalle

Discover the vibrant energy of Place Pigalle, a historic square famous for its lively nightlife, theaters, and the iconic Moulin Rouge.
