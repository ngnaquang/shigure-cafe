In Paris, a Gentle Rain of Coffee & Tea

Shigure (シグレ) is the Japanese word for a passing autumn shower—a brief, quiet moment of reflection. Here, we invite you to find your own moment of calm. Inspired by the elegance of Japanese tea culture and the warmth of Parisian cafés, we offer a space to pause, sip, and savor. Each cup is a tribute to craftsmanship, from our carefully sourced beans to our ceremonial-grade matcha.
